```
E_elec   = η_gen · E_chem            化學能 → 電力
```

$$\boxed{\,N_{token} = \frac{\eta_{gen}}{PUE}\cdot\frac{\kappa}{\varphi}\cdot E_{chem}\,}$$

$$[\text{無量綱}]\cdot\frac{[\text{FLOP/J}]}{[\text{FLOP/token}]}\cdot[\text{J}] = \frac{[\text{token}]}{[\text{J}]}\cdot[\text{J}] = [\text{token}]\;✓$$

$$\varepsilon_{token} = \frac{\varphi}{\kappa}\cdot\frac{PUE}{\eta_{gen}}\quad[\text{J/token}]$$
